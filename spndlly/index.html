Welcome

Thank you for using Spndlly – Expense & Budget Tracker. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information our app collects, how it is used, and your rights regarding your information.

Information We Collect

Spndlly allows you to manage your personal finances by recording:

  • Income
  • Expenses
  • Budgets
  • Accounts
  • Bills
  • Categories
  • Reports
  • Recurring Transactions

All of this information is stored locally on your device. We do not upload your financial records to our servers.

Permissions

  • Notifications – Used for bill reminders and budget alerts.
  • Storage – Used when exporting or importing your data.
  • Camera (Optional) – May be used in future versions for receipt scanning.

Advertising

Spndlly uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. Google may collect information such as your Advertising ID, device information, app interactions, and approximate location to provide relevant ads.

Learn more:
https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

Analytics

To improve stability and performance, anonymous diagnostic information may be collected, including crash reports, app version, Android version, and device model. No financial records or personally identifiable financial information are collected.

How We Use Information

  • Provide app functionality
  • Improve user experience
  • Display advertisements
  • Fix bugs
  • Improve app performance
  • Maintain security

Data Storage

Your financial information remains stored on your device unless you choose to export or back up your data. We do not store your expense history or financial records on our servers.

Third-Party Services

  • Google AdMob
  • Google Play Services
  • Firebase Crashlytics (if enabled)
  • Firebase Analytics (if enabled)
  • Google Play Billing (if applicable)

Google Privacy Policy:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

Children's Privacy

Spndlly is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. However, no electronic storage or transmission method is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

Because your financial information is stored locally on your device, you can delete your information at any time by:

  • Deleting transactions inside the app
  • Clearing app storage
  • Uninstalling the application

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be published on this page with an updated revision date.
