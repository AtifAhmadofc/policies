Privacy Policy

Spndlly – Expense & Budget Tracker

Simple • Secure • Offline First

Last Updated: July 22, 2026
01

Welcome

Thank you for using Spndlly – Expense & Budget Tracker ("Spndlly", "the app", "we", "us"). This Privacy Policy explains what information the app handles, how it is used, and the choices you have. We built Spndlly to be offline-first, meaning your financial data belongs to you and stays on your device by default.

By using Spndlly, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the app.

02

Information We Collect

Spndlly allows you to record and organize your personal finances. The data you choose to enter may include:

  • Income entries
  • Expenses
  • Budgets
  • Bills
  • Accounts
  • Categories
  • Notes
  • Reports
  • Recurring transactions
Your financial information stays on your device. All of the data listed above is stored locally on your Android device and is not transmitted to our servers or any third party by us.
03

Permissions

Spndlly requests a limited set of device permissions, each used only for its stated purpose:

Notifications Used to remind you about upcoming bills, recurring transactions, and budget alerts.
Storage Used to save local backups, export reports, or attach files related to your financial records.
Camera (Optional) Reserved for potential future features, such as scanning receipts. This permission is not required for the app's core functionality and will only be requested if you choose to use such a feature.
04

Advertising

Spndlly uses Google AdMob to display advertisements that help keep the app free to use. To deliver relevant and functioning ads, Google may collect:

  • Advertising ID
  • Device information
  • App interactions
  • Approximate location

This information is collected and processed by Google in accordance with its own policies. For more details, please review Google's Ads Policy at policies.google.com/technologies/ads.

05

Analytics

Spndlly may optionally use Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics to help us understand app performance and fix issues. These tools may collect anonymous diagnostic information, such as:

  • Device model
  • Android version
  • App version
  • Crash reports
Your financial data is never uploaded for analytics. Income, expenses, budgets, accounts, and all other financial entries you create are excluded from analytics and crash reporting.
06

How We Use Information

Any information collected as described above is used to:

  • Improve the app
  • Display advertisements
  • Maintain security
  • Improve performance
  • Fix bugs
  • Provide and support app features
07

Data Storage

All financial information you enter into Spndlly stays on your device. Nothing you record — income, expenses, budgets, bills, accounts, categories, notes, reports, or recurring transactions — is uploaded to our servers.

You are always in control of your own data. If you delete the app or clear its storage, this locally stored information is permanently removed.

08

Third-Party Services

Spndlly relies on the following third-party services to operate:

  • Google Play Services
  • Google AdMob
  • Firebase Analytics
  • Firebase Crashlytics
  • Google Play Billing (if applicable, for in-app purchases)

These services operate under their own privacy policies. You can review Google's Privacy Policy at policies.google.com/privacy.

09

Children's Privacy

Spndlly is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided information through the app, please contact us so we can address the matter.

10

Data Security

We follow reasonable practices to protect the data handled by Spndlly, including keeping financial information stored locally on your device rather than on external servers. However, please note that no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

11

Your Rights

Because your financial data is stored locally, you have direct control over it at all times. You can:

  • Delete individual entries or records within the app
  • Export your data for backup or personal use
  • Clear the app's local storage
  • Uninstall the app, which removes locally stored data from your device
12

Changes To This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal and regulatory reasons. Any updates will be posted on this page along with a revised "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
